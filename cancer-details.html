Cancer Diseases Information

Cancer is a group of diseases characterized by the uncontrolled growth and spread of abnormal cells. Each type of cancer may have different symptoms, precautions, and treatment options. Early detection and timely medical intervention are crucial for effective cancer management.

✤ Brain Cancer

Symptoms

  • Headaches
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Changes in vision and speech

Precautions

  • Regular medical checkups
  • Reduce exposure to radiation (if applicable)
  • Healthy lifestyle choices

Medications

  • Chemotherapy
  • Radiation therapy

✤ Lung Cancer

Symptoms

  • Chronic cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Chest pain

Precautions

  • Avoid smoking and secondhand smoke
  • Regular lung health checkups
  • Promote a smoke-free environment

Medications

  • Chemotherapy
  • Immunotherapy

✤ Stomach Cancer

Symptoms

  • Abdominal pain
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Indigestion

Precautions

  • Healthy diet and nutrition
  • Regular medical checkups
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol

Medications

  • Chemotherapy
  • Surgery (in some cases)

✤ Bone Cancer

Symptoms

  • Bone pain and swelling
  • Frequent fractures
  • Weakened bones

Precautions

  • Regular bone health checkups
  • Physical therapy (if recommended)
  • Healthy lifestyle choices

Medications

  • Chemotherapy
  • Targeted therapy
