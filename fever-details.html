Fever Diseases Information

Fever diseases are conditions that involve elevated body temperature and may manifest with various symptoms. Here is information about different fever-related illnesses, their symptoms, precautions, and treatment options.

✤ Dengue Fever

Symptoms

  • High fever
  • Severe headache
  • Pain behind the eyes

Precautions

  • Eliminate mosquito breeding sites
  • Use mosquito repellents

Medications

  • Rest and hydration
  • Pain relievers (under medical guidance)

✤ Chikungunya

Symptoms

  • High fever
  • Severe joint pain
  • Rash

Precautions

  • Avoid mosquito bites
  • Use mosquito nets

Medications

  • Rest and hydration
  • Pain relievers (under medical guidance)

✤ Chickenpox

Symptoms

  • Itchy rash
  • Fever and fatigue
  • Flu-like symptoms

Precautions

  • Isolate the infected person
  • Keep the rash clean and dry

Medications

  • Antiviral medications (in severe cases)
  • Calamine lotion for itch relief

✤ Malaria

Symptoms

  • Fever, chills, and sweating
  • Fatigue and muscle pain
  • Nausea and vomiting

Precautions

  • Use insect repellents and bed nets
  • Take antimalarial medications (if traveling to affected areas)

Medications

  • Antimalarial drugs (as prescribed)
  • Rest and hydration
