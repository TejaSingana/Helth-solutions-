COVID-19 Information


COVID-19, caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus, is a highly contagious respiratory illness. Common symptoms include fever, cough, shortness of breath, and fatigue. It can be transmitted through respiratory droplets and contact with contaminated surfaces. Preventive measures include wearing masks, practicing social distancing, frequent handwashing, and following general health guidelines.

Symptoms

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Sore throat
  • Headache
  • Chills
  • Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath

Precautions

  • Wear a mask in public places
  • Practice social distancing
  • Frequent handwashing
  • Avoid close contact with sick individuals
  • Clean and disinfect frequently-touched objects and surfaces
  • Stay home when feeling unwell
  • Follow local health guidelines and restrictions

Medications

  • Remdesivir
  • Dexamethasone
  • Hydroxychloroquine
  • Ivermectin
  • Tocilizumab
  • Favipiravir
  • Oseltamivir
  • Lopinavir/Ritonavir
  • Baricitinib
  • Camostat mesilate
