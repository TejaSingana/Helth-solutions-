Skin Diseases Information

Skin diseases are conditions that affect your skin. These diseases may cause rashes, inflammation, itchiness or other skin changes. Some skin conditions may be genetic, while lifestyle factors may cause others. Skin disease treatment may include medications, creams or ointments, or lifestyle changes.

✤ Acne

Symptoms

  • Red, swollen bumps
  • Blackheads and whiteheads
  • Pimples

Precautions

  • Keep the skin clean
  • Avoid squeezing or picking at acne
  • Use non-comedogenic skincare products

Medicines

  • Topical retinoids
  • Antibiotics
  • Oral contraceptives (for females)

✤ Rosacea

Symptoms

  • Facial redness
  • Swollen, red bumps
  • Eye irritation

Precautions

  • Avoid spicy foods and hot drinks
  • Protect your face from the sun
  • Gentle skincare routine

Medicines

  • Topical antibiotics
  • Oral antibiotics
  • Topical steroids

✤ Psoriasis

Symptoms

  • Red patches of skin with silvery scales
  • Dry, cracked skin that may bleed
  • Itching and burning

Precautions

  • Avoid triggers like stress and infections
  • Moisturize regularly
  • Expose your skin to sunlight in moderation

Medicines

  • Topical corticosteroids
  • Systemic medications (oral or injected)
  • Phototherapy

✤ Vitiligo

Symptoms

  • Loss of skin color in patches
  • Premature whitening or graying of hair on the affected areas
  • Sensitivity to sun exposure

Precautions

  • Use sunscreen to protect depigmented areas
  • Camouflage makeup to conceal affected areas
  • Topical corticosteroids for inflammation

Medicines

  • Topical corticosteroids
  • Topical calcineurin inhibitors
  • Pigment grafting

✤ Ringworm

Symptoms

  • Red, itchy, raised patches with a clear center
  • Rash with defined edges
  • Blistering or oozing

Precautions

  • Keep the affected area clean and dry
  • Avoid sharing personal items
  • Use antifungal creams

Medicines

  • Antifungal creams
  • Oral antifungal medications
  • Topical steroids (for inflammation)
